# NiLuJe K5 Jailbreak
The NiLuJe K5 Jailbreak is a legacy jailbreak created by [NiLuJe](https://www.mobileread.com/forums/member.php?u=69624)

## Prerequisites
- Please check that your Kindle is [compatible](../../kindle-models) with the K2 Jailbreak
- You will also need a PC

            <div class="stepContent">

                <p>Copy <b>ALL</b> the files from the extracted zip to your Kindle's root</p>
            </div>
        </div>
        <div class="step">
            <h2>Eject & Unplug</h2>
            <div class="stepContent">
                <p>Eject and unplug your Kindle</p>
            </div>
        </div>
        <div class="step">
            <h2>Perform the jailbreak</h2>
            <div class="stepContent">
                <p>Go to <code>[HOME]</code> -> <code>[MENU]</code> > Settings -> <code>[MENU]</code> > Update Your Kindle</p>
                <p>After a few seconds, you'll see <code>**** JAILBREAK ****</code> at the bottom of your screen, your Kindle is now jailbroken.</p>
                <p class="note">
                    It is recommended you manually reboot your Kindle at this stage to ensure the jailbreak's MKK components are functional:
                    <br>
                    <code>[HOME]</code> -> <code>[MENU]</code> > Settings -> <code>[MENU]</code> > Restart
                </p>
            </div>
